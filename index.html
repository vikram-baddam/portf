ABOUT ME

I am Freelance Senior VFX Artist from Buenos Aires, Argentina, currently based in the heart of Berlin, Germany, since 2014.

VFX are my passion, I started as a Motion Graphics Designer back in 2006, and further developed into a CG Generalist until I became a full time Digital Compositor. Since then, I successfully worked for more than 20 different studios across 2 continents, 6 countries and 10 cities. I am also a teacher for L'IDEM Animation University in Barcelona, giving intensive courses on Compositing for 3rd year students.

My every day tool is Nuke, which I complement with Mocha, Photoshop and After Effects if needed, but I can always jump to Maya for modelling, texturing and lighting (Arnold).

I look forward to getting to know new amazing CG, VFX and Animation studios, and I love to travel to new cities. Please check my availability and/or feel free to get in touch! I can work remotely or of course travel to work in-house within the EU.
